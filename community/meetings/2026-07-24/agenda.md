## Agenda

1. **Approval of Minutes** *(5 min)*
   Review and formal approval of minutes from the most recent prior session.

2. **Nominations and Elections** *(40 min)*
   Formal nominations and voting for open positions across the four working groups — Core Architecture, DataBook Standard, Network Architecture, and Identity — plus the Admin and CG Ontologist roles.
