# W3C Holon Community Group — Inaugural Meeting Agenda

**Date:** Friday, 19 June 2026
**Time:** 7:00–9:00 AM Pacific / 15:00–17:00 GMT / 16:00–18:00 CET
**Location:** Google Meet — https://meet.google.com/set-euqf-ksj
**Chair:** Kurt Cagle (Acting)

---

## Agenda

1. **Introductions** *(~20 min)*
   All participants: name, affiliation, and brief statement of interest in holon architectures. Please keep to 60–90 seconds to allow time for all voices.

2. **Charter Discussion** *(~20 min)*
   Overview of the proposed HCG charter — scope, goals, IP policy, and relationship to W3C processes. The draft charter is available in the GitHub repository at https://github.com/w3c-cg/holon. Comments and questions welcome; formal adoption at a subsequent meeting.

3. **Open Nominations for Chair and Co-Chairs** *(~10 min)*
   The Acting Chair will open nominations for Chair and Co-Chair positions. Nominees should be HCG members willing to serve a one-year term. Nominations accepted today; election by group vote at the next meeting.

4. **HGA Presentation: Holons — An Introduction** *(~25 min)*
   A structured introduction to the Holon Graph Architecture — covering the holon model, the four aspects (Scene Graph, Event Graph, Boundary, Projection Layer), agents, DataBooks, and the rationale for a community standards effort. Slides and accompanying DataBook available at https://github.com/w3c-cg/holon/tree/main/community/working.

5. **Discussions on Active Working Groups** *(~20 min)*
   Overview of five proposed working groups. Participants are invited to express interest and propose additional groups or consolidations. Full proposals will be posted to the public-holon@w3.org mailing list for asynchronous discussion.

   Proposed groups:
   - I — Holon Core Architecture
   - II — DataBooks
   - III — Holon Network Architecture
   - IV — Validation / Verification / Security
   - V — Industry Utilisation

6. **Open Forum** *(~15 min)*
   Open floor for questions, comments, and items not covered by the agenda. Items that cannot be addressed in the time available will be carried to the mailing list.

7. **Closing Remarks** *(~5 min)*
   Summary of actions, next meeting date, and any outstanding items.
